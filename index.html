PORK ADOBO

This is a Spanish inspired dish consisting of pork

Recipe

  1. 2 lbs pork belly or pork shoulder, cut into bite-size pieces
  2. 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. 3 cloves garlic, chopped
  4. ⅓ cup light soy sauce
  5. ⅓ cup vinegar
  6. 1 bay leaf
  7. ½ tsp freshly ground pepper
  8. 1 cup water or more as needed
  9. 1 tbsp honey or brown sugar
